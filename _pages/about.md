## About me {#about}

Hi! I'm Anurag, a second-year master's student in Robotics at Northeastern University. I'm interested in all things Reinforcement Learning. At Northeastern, I work with [Dr. Lawson Wong](https://www.ccs.neu.edu/home/lsw/) on LLM-assisted reinforcement learning and with [Dr. Chris Amato](https://www.ccs.neu.edu/home/camato/) on cooperative multi-agent reinforcement learning.

Before joining Northeastern, I worked on distributional reinforcement learning and neuroscience research at Harvard with [Dr. Naoshige Uchida](https://www.mcb.harvard.edu/directory/naoshige-uchida/) and [Dr. Adam Lowet](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=VIhv0UIAAAAJ&hl=en). I have also worked with [Dr. Hendrik Baier](https://www.hendrikbaier.com/) at CWI and with [Dr. Kristian Kersting](https://ml-research.github.io/people/kkersting/) and [Quentin Delfosse](https://quentindelfosse.me/) at the AIML Lab, TU Darmstadt.
